Data of the People, By the People, For the People

OCDO Mission is to responsibly and ethically enable data-driven health and human services to maximize the nation’s return on investment in its data and benefit the American public.

We achieve this by leveraging OCDO’s enterprise position:

  • Engage 400 data sources and enterprise data sharing
  • Build coalitions and shared decision making
  • Assert OCDO’s capacity to convene and connect people
  • Scout and scale solutions emerging from Operating Divisions
  • Wield authority as OCDO to advance the department’s strategic priorities
